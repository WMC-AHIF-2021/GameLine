What is Ant Attack

Ant Attack is a game from 1983 and its in the 'Jump and Run' genres.
Ant attack takes place in a ruined city dying of giant ants attacked.
The playing figures have the advantage over the ants that they can jump on the ruins.


Goal of the game...

The aim of the game is to find either as a hero the girl or as a heroine the boy
in the city and save them. Once the person has been found,
it must be brought safely out of the city.
When the found person is freed, the person automatically runs alongside or behind the hero,
which increases the degree of difficulty when moving quickly or when climbing.


Predecessor of the game

Zaxxon, a slot machine game from America, was, so to speak, the "predecessor".
But it didn't have real 3D graphics. That's why Ant Attack is the first official 3D game
